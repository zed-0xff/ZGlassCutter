[h1]Glass Cutter[/h1]
A small mod that adds a Glass Cutter tool for [b]safely removing glass panes from windows[/b] without smashing them.

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Cut out glass[/b] — Right-click a window → Window → Cut out glass (requires cutter and Maintenance 1)
[*] [b]Break chance[/b] — Success depends on Maintenance, Glassmaking, Science, Aiming, Woodwork, and Agility; lower chance with higher skills
[*] [b]Engineer bonus[/b] — Engineers get a 50% break chance reduction
[*] [b]Gloves matter[/b] — Wearing awkward gloves or All Thumbs trait increases break chance
[*] [b]Obtaining[/b] — Find the cutter with other tools (world loot) or craft it after finding the recipe magazine
[/list]
